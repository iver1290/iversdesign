Ivers Design

Luke Ivers, AIA, LEED Green Associate
Licensed Architect in Idaho & California

Luke Ivers is an architect and educator with extensive experience in design, project leadership, and creative exploration. He is the founder of Ivers Design, an independent practice launched in 2017, and has worked in senior roles at firms including ELS Architecture & Urban Design, SGPA Architecture, Hummel Architects, and W|A Architects & Engineers. His portfolio includes award-winning projects across typologies such as civic, commercial, mixed-use, and educational design.
